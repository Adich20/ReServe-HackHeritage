Features Workflow

How ReServe connects donors, receivers, and delivery partners.

Sign Up / Login

Receiver registers or logs in to the platform.

Search Surplus

Receivers look for available food donations.

Immediate Requirement

Direct availability check with admin.

Future Requirement

Raise a request and negotiate with donor.

AI/ML + Manual Quality Check

Ensuring food quality and freshness.

Coordinate Pickup

Admins and partners coordinate logistics.

Pickup Successful

Move to Impact Dashboard and feedback loop.

Pickup Failed

Relist items or redirect to shelters/composting.

End Current Listing

Closing the donation cycle and tracking impact.
